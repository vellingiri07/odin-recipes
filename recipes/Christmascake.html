Christmas Cake

Description

A traditional Christmas cake is a rich, moist, and flavorful dessert filled with dried fruits, nuts, and warm spices. Often infused with rum or brandy, it is the centerpiece of holiday celebrations, enjoyed for its decadent taste and festive charm.

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 1 tsp allspice powder
  • 150g raisins
  • 150g currants
  • 100g candied peel
  • 100g chopped dried apricots
  • 100g chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • Zest and juice of 1 orange
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Soak dried fruits, candied peel, and nuts in rum or brandy overnight, adding orange and lemon zest and juice.
  2. Preheat the oven to 150°C (300°F) and line a cake tin with parchment paper.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, beat in eggs one at a time, and mix in vanilla extract.
  4. Sift flour, baking powder, and spices, then fold into the wet mixture.
  5. Add the soaked fruits and nuts, mix well, and pour the batter into the prepared tin.
  6. Bake for 2-2.5 hours or until a skewer comes out clean. Let it cool completely.
  7. Optionally, "feed" the cake with rum weekly until Christmas.
  8. Decorate with marzipan, royal icing, or powdered sugar before serving.
