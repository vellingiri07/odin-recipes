Peanut Butter Biscuits

Description

Peanut butter biscuits are soft, chewy treats made with the rich, creamy flavor of peanut butter. They’re simple to make and perfect for snacking or dessert!

Ingrediens

  1. 1 cup peanut butter
  2. 1 cup sugar
  3. 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Scoop out spoonfuls of dough, roll into balls, and place them on the baking sheet. Flatten each with a fork to create a crisscross pattern.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, let them cool, and enjoy!
